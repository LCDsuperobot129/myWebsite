About RMU

Rooted in History, Solving the Future

At RMU, we envision the future and solve it. With exceptional standards and quality, students constantly excel in their fields, extending the boundaries of human limit. Our legacy dates back all the way to the Napoleonic Era, which is where our motto "Cymru Am Byth!" (Wales Forever!) originates from. This motto was a chant used by Welsh soldiers (most notably the 23rd Royal Welch Fusiliers) fighting alongside the Duke of Wellington. Today, we continue to maintain this spirit and origin for students, teachers, and staff. Join us today!
